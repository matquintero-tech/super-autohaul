FAQ

Is Super AutoHaul a licensed and insured auto transport company?

Yes, Super AutoHaul is fully licensed and insured. We are registered with the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) and carry comprehensive cargo insurance to protect your vehicle throughout the entire transport process.

How do I know my car is safe during pickup and delivery?

When your carrier is confirmed, you'll receive their name, license plate, and vehicle information. It's like knowing your Uber driver — just for your car transport.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and electronic bank transfers. No payment is required until your vehicle is scheduled for pickup, giving you flexibility and peace of mind.

Can I ship personal items in my vehicle?

While we allow up to 100 lbs of personal items in the trunk, we recommend keeping valuables with you. Personal items are not covered by carrier insurance, and excess weight may affect pricing.

How far in advance should I book my transport?

We recommend booking 1-2 weeks in advance for the best carrier availability and rates. However, we also offer expedited shipping for last-minute needs with carriers available within 24-48 hours.
